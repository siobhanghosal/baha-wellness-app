# Product Manager Recommendations

## Immediate PM Priorities

1. Reclassify the current app as a concept prototype, not a product-faithful prototype, until PRR-001 and PRR-002 are resolved.
2. Define a stakeholder-review scope limited to the journeys that can be rebuilt faithfully in the next sprint.
3. Freeze feature expansion until the implementation catches up to the documented UX and navigation model.
4. Convert the 16 review issues into a triaged remediation backlog with owners and acceptance criteria.
5. Require every demo claim to reference either implemented evidence or documented aspiration, never both interchangeably.

## Recommended PM Sequence

### Sprint 1

- Close PRR-001, PRR-002, PRR-003, PRR-004, PRR-006, and PRR-007.
- Pick no more than three stakeholder stories for full fidelity rebuild.
- Define sign-off criteria for concept demo versus review prototype versus implementation reference.

### Sprint 2

- Close PRR-005, PRR-008, PRR-009, PRR-011, PRR-012, and PRR-014.
- Add localization and accessibility acceptance criteria to route-level delivery.
- Align every scenario to a documented route map and role guard.

### Sprint 3

- Close PRR-010, PRR-013, PRR-015, and PRR-016.
- Prepare a design-to-Flutter handoff pack based on corrected screens.

## PM Watchouts

- Do not let the richness of the documentation mask the incompleteness of the prototype.
- Do not let stakeholder-demo needs override privacy and role-boundary expectations.
- Do not let Figma generation start from generic prototype patterns where the UX spec is more precise.
